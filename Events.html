EVENTS

Department hosts a myriad of special events throughout the academic year, creating a dynamic and engaging environment for students, faculty, and industry professionals. From hackathons and coding competitions that fuel creativity and problem-solving skills to distinguished guest lectures by renowned experts in the field, our special events are designed to inspire and connect. Career fairs and industry panels provide students with valuable networking opportunities, bridging the gap between academia and the tech industry. Additionally, research symposiums and showcases celebrate the innovative work of our students and faculty, fostering a culture of collaboration and intellectual exploration. These special events not only enrich the academic experience but also contribute to the vibrant and evolving landscape of the Computer Science Department.
