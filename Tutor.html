STUEDENT TUTORING

The Computer Science Department is thrilled to introduce a student tutoring initiative aimed at fostering a supportive and collaborative learning environment. Recognizing the diverse learning needs of our students, the tutoring program provides an opportunity for peer-to-peer knowledge exchange. Seasoned students, well-versed in various computer science subjects, will offer guidance and assistance to their peers, whether it's clarifying complex concepts, assisting with programming challenges, or providing study tips. This initiative not only enhances academic support but also cultivates a sense of community within the department, reinforcing the idea that learning is a shared journey where everyone can contribute and benefit. We believe that this student tutoring program will be instrumental in promoting academic success and creating a vibrant learning community within our department.We have created this form to help us facilitate the process.
