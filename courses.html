OUR COURSES

Undergraduate Programmes
Earning a top-tier Bachelor of Science in Computer Science degree paves the way for in-demand technology career prospects..
The Department offers two undergraduate degree programmes, Bachelor of Science in Computer Science Single-Major (3:2:1:1) and Major-Minor (3:2:2:1) as well as Bachelor of Science in Information Technology.
To qualify for admission into Computer Science programmes at Level 200, candidates must have taken the following courses at Level 100:
MATH 121: Algebra and Trigonometry
MATH 122: Calculus I CSCD 101: Introduction to Computer Science I
CSCD 102: Introduction to Computer Science II
Laptop with required specifications
MPhil and Msc Programmes
Embrace an exceptional graduate education that integrates state-of-the-art research with practical real-world experience.
The Master of Science (MSc) in Computer Science program offers an immersive and advanced education in the ever-evolving field of computer science. Typically spanning two years, the program combines a blend of theoretical knowledge and practical skills development.
Students engage in a diverse array of coursework covering fundamental concepts such as algorithms, data structures, and software engineering, while also delving into specialized areas like artificial intelligence, machine learning, and cybersecurity. The curriculum often includes hands-on projects, laboratory work, and collaborative research initiatives. Through a combination of lectures, seminars, and practical applications, students gain a deep understanding of the latest developments in computer science and are well-prepared to contribute to industry or pursue further research opportunities upon completion of the program.
PhD Programmes
"The computer science department's PhD program offers cutting-edge research opportunities, fostering innovation and collaboration among students and faculty in the dynamic and rapidly evolving field of computer science."
The computer science department's PhD program is a rigorous and comprehensive academic journey designed to equip students with advanced expertise in the field. The program typically spans four to six years, during which students engage in a combination of coursework, research, and teaching experiences.
The curriculum is tailored to provide a solid foundation in core computer science concepts while allowing for specialization in diverse areas such as artificial intelligence, data science, cybersecurity, and more. Research methods encompass a blend of theoretical exploration and practical application, encouraging students to contribute to the advancement of knowledge through innovative projects and scholarly publications. With a focus on fostering critical thinking and independent inquiry, the program prepares graduates to make significant contributions to academia, industry, and beyond.
